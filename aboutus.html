Alex's Pies

Hello my name is Alex Stitt and I am a 3rd generation pie-maker!

I started this business after taking up baking as a hobby during the COVID-19 pandemic. We strive to offer the best pies in Evanston and offer a wide selection. We have three guiding principles for our pies:

  1. All natural ingredients, all the time
  2. Crust is king
  3. Quality over quantity
